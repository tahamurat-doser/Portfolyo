About Me

Sith Lord and Commander of the Death Star

Hello, I am Darth Vader. I am the most powerful and loyal servant of the Galactic Empire. I am also the Sith Lord, commander of the Death Star and Emperor Palpatine's right hand man. My goal is to subjugate the galaxy to the will of the Empire and destroy the rebel forces. I was born Anakin Skywalker and raised as a slave. I was discovered by the Jedi master Qui-Gon Jinn and trained as a Jedi. As a Jedi, I served the Republic in the Clone Wars and won many victories. But at the same time, I was drawn to the dark side of the force. Fearing the loss of my wife Padme Amidala, I became a disciple of the Sith Lord Palpatine and attacked the Jedi Temple. During this time, I was severely injured in a fight with my former master Obi-Wan Kenobi and needed mechanical armor to survive. Since then, as Darth Vader, I have been carrying out the Emperor's orders, fighting against the Rebel Alliance and trying to find their leader, Luke Skywalker. When I learned that Luke Skywalker was my son, I tried to convince him to turn to the dark side and defy the Emperor. But he chose the Jedi path and rejected me. Finally, on the Death Star, when I saw the Emperor trying to kill him, I killed the Emperor and sacrificed myself to save him. Thus, I saved both my son and the galaxy and returned to the light side of the force. If you, too, want to bring the galaxy under the rule of the Empire, contact me. Let me show you how attractive the dark side of power can be.
